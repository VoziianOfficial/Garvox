Cookie Policy

How cookies and browser storage may be used

This Cookie Policy applies to the GARVOX website. It explains basic browser storage and policy confirmation behavior in clear terms.

1. Platform role

GARVOX is an independent provider-matching platform for garage door service categories. It does not perform garage door installation, replacement, repair, opener, or related services directly.

2. What cookies are

Cookies are small files or pieces of browser data that may be stored on a visitor’s device when visiting a website. Browser storage can also include localStorage, which is used by many websites to remember simple choices.

3. Policy banner storage

This website includes a policy confirmation banner with Accept and Decline buttons. When a visitor makes a selection, the choice may be stored in localStorage so the banner does not continue appearing on every page.

This choice is used only to remember the visitor’s policy banner preference on this website.

4. Types of storage this site may use

This website may use essential browser storage for policy confirmation, basic website functionality, preference retention, and user experience consistency.

Additional analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking tools should not be assumed unless they are specifically added to the website code.

5. Managing cookies and storage

Visitors can usually manage cookies and localStorage through browser settings. Clearing browser data may remove saved policy banner choices and cause the banner to appear again.

6. Third-party websites

This website may link to other websites or independent provider resources. Third-party websites may use their own cookies, tracking technologies, privacy practices, and terms.

GARVOX does not control third-party websites or their cookie practices.

7. Provider verification

Any provider-related information, including availability, pricing, license, insurance, warranties, schedule, and project details, must be verified directly with the provider before hiring.

8. Updates to this Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the website after updates means you accept the revised policy.

9. Contact

Questions about this Cookie Policy may be directed to contact@garvox.com.

Policy banner behavior

The banner stays until a choice is selected.

The GARVOX policy confirmation banner appears at the bottom of the website on first visit. It includes Accept and Decline buttons plus links to Privacy Policy, Cookie Policy, and Terms of Service.
