Why GARVOX exists

Garage door projects are mechanical, local, and detail-heavy.

Homeowners often need to compare materials, opener compatibility, quote scope, warranty terms, and availability before contacting a company. GARVOX gives that comparison process a cleaner structure.

The platform was designed around real garage-door decision points: the door system, the track, the opener, the material, the condition of existing hardware, and the provider’s service-area fit.
