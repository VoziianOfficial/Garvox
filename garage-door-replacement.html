What to ask providers

Replacement quotes should be compared by scope, not just headline price.

A clear replacement comparison helps homeowners understand removal, disposal, door selection, insulation, opener compatibility, warranty, and provider availability.

01

Old system removal

Ask whether the quote includes removal of the existing door, hardware, or related components.

02

Disposal details

Confirm whether haul-away, disposal, or cleanup is included or priced separately.

03

Door material

Compare steel, aluminum, wood-look, glass, insulated, and other available replacement options.

04

Insulation

Ask how insulation affects comfort, door weight, noise, opener compatibility, and quote scope.

05

Opener fit

Confirm whether the existing opener can be used or whether a different opener should be compared.

06

Warranty

Review material, hardware, opener, and project warranty details directly with providers.
