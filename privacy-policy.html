Privacy Policy

How this website handles information

This Privacy Policy applies to the GARVOX website and related provider-matching content. It is written to explain the platform model in clear, readable language.

1. Platform role

GARVOX is an independent garage door provider-matching platform. GARVOX does not install, replace, repair, inspect, maintain, or service garage doors directly.

The website helps homeowners compare garage door service categories, review provider-related questions, and prepare to contact independent local providers directly.

2. Information you may provide

When using the contact form or contacting GARVOX, you may choose to provide information such as your name, phone number, email address, selected service category, and project details.

You should avoid submitting sensitive personal information through the contact form unless it is necessary for the request.

3. How information may be used

Information submitted through the website may be used to understand the request, respond to inquiries, support provider-comparison workflows, improve website content, and maintain website functionality.

Any provider-related information, availability, pricing, licensing, insurance, warranty, schedule, or project scope must be verified directly with the provider before hiring.

4. Provider verification

Homeowners are responsible for verifying provider license, insurance, quote details, warranty terms, pricing, schedule, service area, and all other hiring details directly with providers.

GARVOX does not warrant or guarantee provider work.

5. Cookies and local storage

This website may use basic browser storage, such as localStorage, to remember policy banner choices. Additional cookie-related details are explained in the Cookie Policy.

You can control cookies and browser storage through your browser settings.

6. Third-party links

This website may include links to service pages, legal pages, contact options, or independent provider resources. Third-party websites are not controlled by GARVOX.

If you visit a third-party website or communicate with a provider, that third party’s own policies and practices may apply.

7. Data security

Reasonable efforts may be used to support website security. However, no website, email system, contact form, or electronic communication method can be guaranteed to be fully secure.

8. Children’s privacy

This website is intended for general homeowner and adult audience use. It is not directed to children.

9. Policy updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the website after updates means you accept the revised policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy may be directed to contact@garvox.com.
